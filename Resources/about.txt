Welcome to SkateMaps

Finally a skatepark finder app designed by skaters for
skaters!

HOW TO USE THIS APP
From the home screen click LIST PARKS to view
a list of skateparks. 
VIEW LARGER IMAGES by clicking on the image.
GET DIRECTIONS by clicking on the drop pin or 
address.
 
 
From the home screen click MAP VIEW to view nearby
skateparks on a map. 
Click on a drop pin to see park name and address
GET DIRECTIONS by clicking the info button in the
top left.
