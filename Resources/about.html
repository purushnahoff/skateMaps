Welcome to SkateMaps

Finally an easy to use skatepark finder app that gives you directions. Designed and built by skaters for skaters!

Listing Skateparks

From the home screen simply click the LIST PARKS button to open the list of skateparks. The list of parks are ordered by distance from your current location (closest to furthest)
Click on a park image to view a selection of larger images.
GET DIRECTIONS to the park by simply clicking the address or red drop pin marker to launch google maps.

Alternativly from the home screen click MAP VIEW to view nearby skateparks on a map. Click on a drop pin to see park name and address.
Get more detail and directions by clicking the info button.

Search Feature

Supports filtering parks by name, suburb, city, state (QLD,NSW,VIC etc..) and rating (3 stars, 5 stars etc..).
